Pizza

Description

A classic Italian dish with a crispy crust, tomato sauce, melted cheese, and toppings of your choice.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking tray.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
