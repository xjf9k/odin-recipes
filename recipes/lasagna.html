Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian lasagna made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese, baked to golden perfection.A classic Italian lasagna made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese, baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • - 9 lasagna noodles
  • - 500g ground beef
  • - 2 cups tomato sauce
  • - 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • - 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • - 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • - Salt, pepper, oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, cook beef and onion until browned. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and oregano. Simmer 10 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  7. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
