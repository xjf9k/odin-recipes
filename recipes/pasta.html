Pasta

Description

A simple and versatile Italian dish made with boiled pasta and your favorite sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce or cream sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© garlic.
  3. Add sauce (tomato or cream) and simmer.
  4. Mix pasta with the sauce.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan.
